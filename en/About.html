About

Along the Blades is a site dedicated to exploring swords, their symbolic meanings, and their representations in history and fiction. From the mythical sword Excalibur to the futuristic Quantum Blade, we analyze how these weapons have traversed the ages embodying power, honor, and destiny. Through articles on cinema, literature, and video games, our mission is to show how the sword remains a timeless universal symbol.

The sword is often seen as a cold and bloodthirsty weapon. Through this site, we wanted to highlight the blade of this weapon, to show that it is not just a tool for combat, but that it indeed has a history.

The sword is a symbol of life for some people and at certain times. It is important to highlight the symbols it can represent for some blade holders. Honor, destiny, royalty, divinity, and much more, these are all signs of greatness and importance that this feared tool carries.

The atmosphere we wanted to give to the site is a cold or even icy atmosphere married to a heroic and mystical ambiance. The colors and typographies are a strategic choice that we have attributed to the site.
